# What is Trisul AI (UI)?

**Trisul AI (UI)** is an AI-powered assistant integrated into the Trisul Network Analytics web interface that helps users explore telemetry, investigate network activity, and access analytics through natural-language interactions.

Traditional network analytics platforms often require users to know where information is located and how different views, dashboards, and workflows are organized. Trisul AI (UI) reduces this navigation overhead by allowing users to begin with a question and be guided toward the relevant data.

Rather than replacing existing analytical workflows, Trisul AI (UI) provides a conversational layer that helps users interact with network telemetry more naturally.

---

## Why Trisul AI (UI) matters
Modern networks generate large volumes of telemetry, flow records, alerts, and historical data. Finding the right information quickly can be challenging, especially during troubleshooting or investigations.

Trisul AI (UI) helps bridge the gap between user questions and network data. Instead of manually navigating multiple dashboards or views, users can interact with the platform conversationally to explore traffic behavior, investigate activity, understand network conditions, and access relevant analytics.

This approach can reduce the effort required to locate information and help users focus more on understanding network behavior than on navigating the platform itself.

---

## How Trisul AI (UI) works
Users interact with Trisul AI (UI) through natural-language questions within the web interface.

The assistant helps users explore telemetry, navigate related views, access historical information, investigate hosts and applications, and understand network activity through conversational interactions.

It can also help users discover workflows, visualizations, and analytical capabilities that they may not otherwise know exist, making the platform more accessible to both new and experienced users.

---

## Trisul AI (UI) vs Trisul AI (CLI)
| Category | Trisul AI (UI) | Trisul AI (CLI) |
|---|---|---|
| Primary interface | Web interface | Command-line interface |
| Interaction style | Natural-language conversations | Terminal-based interactions |
| Primary workflow | Interactive exploration and investigations | Querying, scripting, and automation |
| Best fit | Visual operational workflows | Automation-oriented workflows |
| User experience | Guided graphical interaction | Power-user and automation workflows |

Both approaches complement each other and support different operational needs.

---

## In Trisul
Trisul AI (UI) is designed to work alongside Trisul's existing monitoring, visualization, and investigation capabilities.

Rather than replacing dashboards, FlowMap visualizations, historical analysis, or investigative workflows, it acts as a conversational layer that helps users access and navigate them more efficiently. This enables analysts to move from questions to relevant telemetry with less manual effort and greater contextual guidance.

For additional information, refer to the Trisul documentation.
